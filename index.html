About us

At Legacy Insurance Management, our unwavering commitment lies in providing our clients with adept and
innovative guidance to effectively address their specific insurance requirements.

  • Our main focus is always our clients, and we prioritize your interests. We’re committed to providing a complete insurance service. This means we study and understand the risks our clients and their businesses face, so we can create insurance plans that fit them perfectly.
  • We work for you, not the insurance company. Our goal is to make sure you’re well protected, and we take personal care to find the right insurance that matches what you need and what you can afford. We have connections with over 100 of Australia’s top insurance companies. As your professional broker, we represent you, the client – this means we help you get the best products and services at the best prices for your insurance needs.
  • We might not be the biggest, but we want to do the best for our clients. We offer tailored products with good coverage at good prices, along with excellent professional service, including help with claims. Our job isn’t just selling insurance; it’s about giving advice and service to help manage risks and bring peace of mind.
